# Knowledge: Kemindo Group — Company Profile

> Source: public information from Kemindo websites + business directories (kemindogroup.com,
> kemindo.id, kemindo.goldsupplier.com, LinkedIn). For the customer-facing Solution Advisor.

## Who is Kemindo

Kemindo Group positions itself as **"Your Solution Partner®"** — an industrial solution
provider, not just a chemical distributor. Customers come with operational problems
(gold recovery, paper brightness, nickel reagents, biomass energy) and Kemindo provides
products + technical know-how to solve them.

- Established on the chemicals business (started with **bentonite and lime** series), since ~2008–2009.
- Headquarters: **Jakarta, Indonesia**, with presence in **Singapore**.
- Contact: **marketing@kemindogroup.com** · kemindogroup.com

## Business areas

1. **Chemical** — specialty & industrial chemicals. Main industries served: **paper, gold mining,
   alumina smelter, nickel mining**, and general chemicals. Product families include lime
   (StarHLime), bentonite/paper "Star" line (StarLITE, StarSORB, StarPOL, StarMPOL, STARFIX),
   activated carbon, caustic soda, sulfuric acid, flotation reagents, grinding media, and more.
2. **Logistics** — "one-stop" sea logistics (barge & vessel), trucking, and warehouse management.
3. **Energy** — since 2013: started in coal trading, now operates its own coal mine in **Riau**,
   and supplies **Palm Kernel Shell (PKS)** biomass for green energy.
4. **Agriculture** — native starch, crude palm oil (CPO), and organic fertilizer.

## Group structure (entities)

- **PT Kemindo International** — specialty & industrial chemicals; Jakarta + Singapore.
- **PT Kemindo Artha Jaya** (est. 2014) — agriculture, animal feed, pulp & paper, textile,
  oil & gas mining sectors.
- **PT Kemindo Jaya Prima** — plating / metal surface finishing chemicals + screen printing
  supplies (authorized for brands such as Growel and Matsui).
- **Energy & Logistics divisions** as above.

## Milestones

- 2008–2009: established in specialty/industrial chemicals.
- 2013: entered energy (coal trading → own coal mine, PKS biomass).
- 2014: first **hydrated lime plant in North Sulawesi**, serving gold mines in East Indonesia;
  PT Kemindo Artha Jaya established.

## Why customers choose Kemindo

- Solution-oriented: technical recommendation + dosage, not just product supply.
- Production sites located near mining areas (e.g. lime near gold mines) for reliable supply.
- One-stop: chemicals + logistics + energy across multiple industries.
- Customer-oriented, committed to continuous improvement.
