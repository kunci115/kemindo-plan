# Knowledge: Gold Recovery (CIL/CIP) Troubleshooting

> Source: industry-standard metallurgy (public knowledge), mapped to Kemindo product line.
> For demo/RAG. Validate dosage against site metallurgist + product SDS before field use.

## Common problem: "Gold recovery turun"

Root-cause checklist (typical CIL/CIP plant):

1. **pH out of range** — leach pH should sit ~10.5–11.5. Low pH → cyanide loss as HCN (volatilization + safety).
   - Reagent: **Hydrated Lime Ca(OH)2 (StarHLime, P001)** or **Quick Lime CaO (P002)**.
   - Guide: 0.5–2.0 kg lime per ton ore to hold target pH.
2. **Low dissolved oxygen** — leaching needs O2. Check sparging/oxygen addition.
3. **Carbon activity / loading** — fouled or under-dosed carbon.
   - Reagent: **Activated Carbon (P003)**, coconut shell, iodine no. ≥1000, 15–25 g/L in tanks.
4. **Preg-robbing / carbonaceous ore** — competing adsorption.
5. **Insufficient cyanide** — check titration; make-up with caustic-stabilized cyanide.
   - pH carrier: **Caustic Soda (P005/P006/P007)**.
6. **Grind too coarse (P80)** — liberation poor.
   - Consumable: **Grinding Balls forged/cast (P013/P014)** — check media size + wear rate.

## Related needs

- **Flotation (sulphide/refractory ore)**: collector **SIPX Xanthate (P012)** 20–100 g/t; activator **Copper Sulphate (P004)** 50–200 g/t.
- **Merrill-Crowe precipitation**: **Aluminum Powder (P022)** alt to zinc.
- **Cyanide detox (tailings/effluent)**: **SMBS (P010)** SO2/air route, or **Hydrogen Peroxide (P011)**.
- **Tailings dewatering**: **PAM flocculant (P025)** 20–150 g/t solids.
- **Fluorine in process water**: **CMC Fluorine Mitigation (P029)**.
- **Eco / cyanide-free**: **Green Lixiviant (P028)** — trial required, ore-specific.

## Cross-sell logic (revenue)

A gold-mine lime customer is a natural buyer of: activated carbon, SMBS detox, grinding media, flocculant. Single-reagent accounts = upsell target.
