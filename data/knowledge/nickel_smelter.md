# Knowledge: Nickel Processing & Smelter Reagents

> Source: industry-standard (public), mapped to Kemindo line. Demo/RAG. Validate before field use.

## Two main nickel routes in Indonesia

### 1. RKEF / Pyrometallurgy (smelter, e.g. Morowali/Weda Bay)
- **Flux / slag chemistry**: **Quick Lime CaO (P002)** — CaO ≥85%, controls slag basicity.
- **Reductant / electrodes**: **Electrode Paste (P016)** for Soderberg SAF; **Graphite Electrode (P017)** for EAF.
- **Refractory lining**: **Low Cement Castable (P015)**, Al2O3 60–80%, up to 1600°C — reline projects.
- **Desulphurization**: **Calcium Carbide CaC2 (P018)**.

### 2. HPAL / Hydrometallurgy (battery-grade Ni, MHP)
- **Leaching acid**: **Sulfuric Acid H2SO4 98% (P008)** — ~250–400 kg per ton dry ore. High-volume contract item.
- **Acid plant sulphur feed**: **Sulphur Granular (P019)**.
- **Neutralization**: **Hydrated/Quick Lime (P001/P002)**, **Caustic Soda Liquid (P007)**.
- **Metal recovery / impurity removal**: **Ion Exchange Resin (P026)**, **PAM flocculant (P025)**.

## Cross-sell logic (revenue)

HPAL plants are massive sulfuric-acid + sulphur consumers → recurring high-value contracts. A castable/refractory sale opens recurring reline + electrode supply. Bundle: acid + sulphur + lime + flocculant.
