# Knowledge: Paper Chemistry (Wet End + Functional)

> Source: industry-standard (public), mapped to Kemindo "Star" brand line. Demo/RAG. Validate before field use.

## Problem: "Paper brightness menurun"

1. **OBA under-dosed / quenched** — **Optical Brightening Agent (P036)**, stilbene di/tetra/hexa-sulphonated. 2–15 kg/ton to target brightness. Note: OBA needs carrier whiteness; di-sulpho vs hexa-sulpho choice depends on furnish.
2. **Anionic trash loading** kills cationic additives → fix with **STARFIX (P042)** poly-DADMAC fixative.
3. **Filler/coating brightness** — **Calcined Clay (P040)** brightness ≥90, **Ground CaCO3 (P044)**.

## Problem: "Retention & drainage drop"

Microparticle retention system (classic dual/micro):
- **Cationic retention aid StarPOL (P034)** 0.3–1.5 kg/ton +
- **Retention Bentonite StarLITE (P030)** 1–4 kg/ton, or
- **Micropolymer StarMPOL (P033)** 0.2–1 kg/ton.

## Problem: "Pitch / stickies deposit"
- **Special Bentonite StarSORB PNP (P031)** — pitch & stickies adsorption.

## Other functional needs
- **Internal sizing**: **ASA (P035)** 0.5–2 kg/ton.
- **Strength**: **Wet Strength PAE (P037)**, **Dry Strength (P038)**, **Native Starch (P039)** surface size 10–40 kg/ton.
- **Bulk / fiber-cost reduction**: **StarBULK (P032)**.
- **Coating binder**: **Latex (P041)**.

## Cross-sell logic (revenue)

The "Star" line is a system, not single products — selling StarPOL pulls StarLITE/StarMPOL. Brightness complaint = entry for OBA + fixative + filler bundle. Paper mills = high-frequency recurring orders → ideal for auto-quote.
